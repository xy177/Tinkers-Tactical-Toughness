This mod aims to add a collection of whimsical Tinkers' Construct tools. Currently, the focus is on defense and close-quarters combat, with plans to expand into other directions in the future.

The reason for this mod's existence is that I originally intended to create a fork of Tinkers' Defense. However, due to its massive scale and several unfinished items, it proved to be beyond my current capabilities. Consequently, I cancelled the fork and decided to develop a brand-new mod to carry on the original vision. While the initial content remains defense-oriented, it will explore different design philosophies — I hope everything goes smoothly.

Current Plans:

1. (Finished) Added two types of shields with distinct roles and functions that set them apart from the Battlesign;
2. (Finished) Migrate the Tinkers' version of Nunchaku into this mod;
3. Potentially add new types of armor (depending on my technical capabilities);
4. No plans to add new materials for now; whether to add new traits will be decided on a case-by-case basis (as many excellent mods already offer a wealth of creative materials and traits).

Please note: Portions of this mod were created with AI assistance (for brainstorming and problem-solving). All relevant parts have been manually verified and reviewed by a human developer.
